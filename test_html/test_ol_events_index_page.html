Open Learning hosts a wide range of events for learners, educators, researchers, and practitioners around the world.

Regular event series include Open Learning Talks, which bring together leaders to discuss research-based ideas, technologies, and efforts in education; and xTalks, which provide MIT faculty, researchers, staff, and students an opportunity to share their experiences developing and using digital technologies in the classroom.

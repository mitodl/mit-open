Automation in the financial sector

March 20, 2024 12:00pm
Register for the webinar>>
Location
Online
Type
Webinar
Audience
Faculty
MIT Community
Public
Students

With the explosion of AI–related technologies, financial services firms are embracing intelligent automation — and facing the risks it poses for them and their employees. Javier Arriaga, an automation engineer in the financial services field, will discuss how much of the work in the sector can be automated, how much should be automated, and what consequences we may see in the coming years.
